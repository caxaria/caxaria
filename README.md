### About

Engineering leader with 16+ years of experience across finance, startups, and tech. Currently leading AI engineering at **Coverflex**, building intelligent systems for the future of employee benefits.

Previously **CTO at [Codacy](https://www.codacy.com/)**, where I led engineering and product teams building automated code review at scale. Before that, I worked in investment banking at **Citibank** and **Credit Suisse** in London, then moved back to Lisbon to build startups. Won the **PITCH competition at WebSummit 2014**.
